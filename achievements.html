Major Achievements

Over the years, Pakistan has won several major cricket trophies. Here are the biggest ones:

Tournament Year Result
Cricket World Cup (ODI) 1992 🏆 Champions
ICC World Twenty20 2009 🏆 Champions
ICC Champions Trophy 2017 🏆 Champions
Cricket World Cup (ODI) 1999 🥈 Runners-up
ICC World Twenty20 2007 🥈 Runners-up

Did You Know?

  • Pakistan was the first Asian team to win the Cricket World Cup.
  • Pakistan is the only team to have won all three ICC white-ball trophies (World Cup, T20 World Cup, and Champions Trophy).
  • Pakistan has produced some of the fastest bowlers in cricket history.
