Welcome!

This website is about the Pakistan National Cricket Team, also known as the "Men in Green" or "Shaheens". Cricket is the most popular sport in Pakistan, and the national team has a proud history of exciting wins, legendary players, and passionate fans all over the world.

Quick Facts

Team Name Pakistan National Cricket Team
Nickname Men in Green / Shaheens
Governing Body Pakistan Cricket Board (PCB)
Team Colours Green and White
World Cup Wins 1992 (ODI), 2009 (T20)
